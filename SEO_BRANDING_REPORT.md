## 2. Analysis of Your Options

### Option A: `fromlocaltoglobal.ai` / `.app`
*   **Length:** 17 characters (Very Long) ðŸ”´
*   **Memorability:** Low. Users will likely misspell it or forget the "to" or "from".
*   **SEO Impact:** While it contains keywords ("Local", "Global"), search engines prefer shorter, punchier domains. It looks spammy to some users.
*   **Verdict:** **Avoid.** It is too long for a primary brand domain. It works better as a marketing slogan than a URL.

### Option B: `totheglobal.ai` / `.app`
*   **Length:** 11 characters (Good) ðŸŸ¢
*   **Memorability:** Moderate. It's easy to spell.
*   **Branding:** It implies movement and progress.
*   **Verdict:** **Strong Contender.** It is the better choice of the two phrases.

### Extension Battle: `.ai` vs `.app`
*   **`.ai` (Artificial Intelligence):**
    *   **Pros:** Extremely trendy, immediately tells the user "This tool uses AI". Builds trust for tech products. High resale value.
    *   **Cons:** More expensive renewal fee (~$80-100/year).
    *   **Recommendation:** **Go with `.ai`** if you want to position this as a premium AI tool.
*   **`.app` (Application):**
    *   **Pros:** Cheaper, implies it's a tool/software. Secure (requires HTTPS).
    *   **Cons:** Less "prestige" than .ai in the current market.
    *   **Recommendation:** Good backup, but `.ai` is stronger for your specific niche.

## 3. SEO & Branding Recommendations

If you are open to alternatives, consider names that are **Action-Oriented** or **Outcome-Oriented**.

### Strategy 1: The "Global" Focus (Your current direction)
If the core value is "Going Global", try to combine "Global" with a word implying readiness or career.
*   **`GlobalReady.ai`** (Strong, clear value proposition)
*   **`BeGlobal.ai`** (Short, action-oriented)
*   **`GlobalCV.ai`** (SEO Goldmine, likely expensive or taken)
*   **`GoGlobal.ai`** (Classic, punchy)

### Strategy 2: The "Career/CV" Focus
Since the tool fixes CVs, having "CV" or "Career" in the name helps with immediate SEO understanding.
*   **`CareerLift.ai`**
*   **`CVBoost.ai`**
*   **`ResumeGlobal.ai`**

### Strategy 3: Abstract & Brandable
These require more marketing but are unique.
*   **`Kariyer.ai`** (If targeting Turkey specifically, this is powerful. "GlobalKariyer" is also excellent for the TR market).
*   **`Velox.ai`** (Latin for speed)
*   **`NexStep.ai`**
